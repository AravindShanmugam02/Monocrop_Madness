1. W, S, A, D - Movement
2. Mouse - Look around
3. U - Shop open, Click on the shop icon to close.
4. I - Inventory, Click on the inventory icon to close.
5. Left Mouse Button - Select
6. Right Mouse Button - Drop item in equiped slot (in inventory)
7. Esc - Pause
8. E - Interact
9. Q - Stop Interact
10. T - Tips
